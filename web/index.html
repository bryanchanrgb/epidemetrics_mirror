Time series of the Blavatnik NPI stringency index

The time series of the stringency index stratified by the countries position in the epidemic are shown in the lines with the mean values shown in the thicker lines. The substantial variation in the stringency suggests that while there does appear to be a significany signal here, it is not substantial.

Select a particular epidemic wave state classification to highlight by clicking on the corresponding legend entry. This shows that there are very few entries in the 0th and 4th class. But beyond that, there isn't a whole heap to say aside from a slight trend towards greater restrictions among earlier stage countries and that there is a clear pattern of rapidly increasing stringency just before T0 is reached. There are only a few exceptions to this rule.
